Songs of My Life

Core Concept

Songs of My Life is based on five significant moments in Shawn's life, transformed into game scenes set in Tokyo, Matsu, a fantasy island, a bedroom, and a server room. Each scene reflects a chapter of his story, paired with his favorite five songs, weaving a narrative that combines personal memories and music into an interactive experience.

Technical Details

The project uses Maya to model Tokyo Tower and a ferry, with Unity bringing the interactive game to life. Shawn scripted all game mechanics, including fixed camera views, side panning with QE, story playback with T, and switching between the five stories using keys 1-5. Recorded narration enriches the gameplay experience.
