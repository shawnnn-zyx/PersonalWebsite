KingdomClash

Core Concept

KingdomClash is an interactive event designed to immerse and engage young players with Three Kingdoms Tactics. Spearheaded by Shawn during his time at Alibaba, the project condensed core gameplay elements into competitive events. It aimed to acquire younger users by exploring innovative interactions between the product and its audience, combining strategy, conflict, and dynamic experiences.
