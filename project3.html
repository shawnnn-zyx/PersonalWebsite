The Great Exploration

Core Concept

The Great Exploration reflects humanity's adventurous spirit intertwined with greed and violence. Set in a near-future led by Elon Musk's SpaceX, it follows Mars colonization efforts. However, human greed is overlooked, leading to territorial and resource wars among settlers. The conflict ends with only one survivor, portraying a dark side of humanity's pursuit of progress.

Technical Details

The project was created using Runway, leveraging generative AI for visuals and voiceovers. Inspired by the video game Fallout and the film The Martian, these influences shaped its aesthetic. Final editing and soundtrack composition were completed in Adobe Premiere, blending AI-generated content and traditional editing to craft this vision of Mars exploration.
