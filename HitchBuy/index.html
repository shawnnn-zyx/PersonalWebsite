How HitchBuy Works

For Wishers (Buyers)

  1. List the product you want to get.
  2. Meet the traveler (HitchBuyer) before purchasing (optional for complex items).
  3. Receive your item by your requested date.

For HitchBuyers (Travelers)

  1. Find a wish that fits your flight destination.
  2. Meet the wisher before purchasing (optional for complex items).
  3. Deliver the item when you fly back.
