Afterlife Package

Core Concept

Afterlife envisions a future where consciousness can be uploaded to a virtual world. This product allows users to preselect Afterlife Packages to customize their digital existence. From luxurious villas to AI companions like chefs and architects, Afterlife offers limitless opportunities for an extraordinary second life, immersing users in a personalized, vibrant virtual reality after death.

Technical Details

Built with Unreal Engine 5, Afterlife creates a stunning virtual world tailored to users' desires. Packages are designed with customizable content, offering diverse experiences. Shawn also produced a promotional video for the product using Adobe Premiere Pro, showcasing its potential as a cutting-edge digital service for immersive second lives.
