Embers of the Palace

Core Concept

Rediscover a lost icon of innovation and imagination. Embers of the Palace invites visitors to journey back to 1853 through digital reconstruction and interactive exploration. It showcases the grandeur of the New York Crystal Palace, a breathtaking symbol of America¡¦s industrial ambitions. With the aid of AR-guided tours and physical models, visitors investigate the mysterious 1858 fire that tragically destroyed the landmark.

Technical Details

The New York Crystal Palace was digitally reconstructed using Blender. PLA material sliced structures were then printed using an Ultimaker S3. The exhibit is displayed above a flat TV screen, which plays 1850s street scenes created in Unreal Engine to enhance visitor immersion. Scanning a QR code allows for 3D model viewing on the Sketchfab platform. In the future, the model will be presented via AR at Bryant Park, the original site of the New York Crystal Palace.
